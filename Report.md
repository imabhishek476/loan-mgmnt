## Advanced Reporting & Analytics

The reporting module will be expanded to deliver actionable, company-wise, and year-wise insights. Reports will be accessible via the admin panel and exportable to Excel and PDF.

---

1) Fraudulent Loan Report

- View fraudulent, lost, or dropped loans.

- Filters Available:
    -- Company (Claim Advance, Capital First Group, Gemini Funds)
    -- Loan status
    -- Year (issue year or detection year)

- Summary and detailed views:
    -- Total fraudulent amount
    -- Number of affected loans
    -- Client-level drill-down

2) Yearly Reports

- Company-wise yearly profit calculation and transaction summaries.
- Transaction Summaries.
- Includes:
    -- Interest income
    -- Administrative and processing fees
    -- Broker fees
    -- Total loan amount
    -- Net profit summary
- Year selection with comparison across multiple years.
- Exportable for accounting and audit purposes.

3) Broker Fee Report

- Company-wise fee tracking.

- Displays:
    -- Number of loans
    -- Total broker fees earned
    -- Loan amounts associated with each company

- Filters
    -- Date range
    -- Company

---

4) Additional Reporting Capabilities (Optional)

- Active vs closed loan summaries
- Loan aging reports
- Outstanding balance reports
- Company income breakdown (interest vs fees)
- Client loan history reports
- Custom filters, sorting, and export options for all reports
